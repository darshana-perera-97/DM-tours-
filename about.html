Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind DM Tours Sri Lanka

How It All Began

Founded in 2010 by passionate travel enthusiasts, DM Tours Sri Lanka was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to share the authentic beauty and rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka with the world.

What started as a small family business has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted tour operators, serving thousands of satisfied travelers from around the globe.
